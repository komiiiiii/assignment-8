    • Trench

    Trench is the fifth studio album by Twenty One Pilots. It is a concept album that serves as a continuation of the story from Blurryface, exploring themes of mental health, suicide, and doubt within a fictional world called Dema and the surrounding continent, "Trench". The album was written by Tyler Joseph and produced by him, with Paul Meany also contributing to the production and songwriting.
